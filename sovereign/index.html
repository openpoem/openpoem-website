By introduction only

Sovereign intelligence, for those who cannot afford to be seen.

A private artificial intelligence that knows your world and answers only to you. Owned outright. Seen by no one.

The principle

Your world should never leave your hands.

Every deal, every record, every word you write is yours. The moment it touches a public AI, it leaves you. It is read, stored, and used to train someone else's model. We do the opposite. Your intelligence lives where you live. Behind your walls. Under your name. Off the grid.

What we build

An intelligence that serves you alone.

A private model, trained on your own world. Your family office, your investments, your correspondence, your archive. It understands all of it, and it answers to no one else. No subscription. No provider. No one looking over your shoulder. An asset you own, not a service you rent.

Discretion

A small number of principals.

We take on very few. Every engagement begins under a non-disclosure agreement. The model is built on hardware you control, or in a vault we host in your name. Nothing is ever shared, sold, or seen.

Access

This work moves by introduction.

We do not advertise it. It passes from one trusted hand to the next. If you were pointed here, you already hold the key.
