Pooled, not exposed

Pool your compute. Keep your walls.

Trusted nodes — your own sites, or a federation of vetted partners — pool their GPUs into one private AI fabric. You gain utilisation, burst capacity and resilience, while every request stays inside the trust boundary.

Higher utilisation

Idle GPUs across sites become one shared pool instead of dead capacity.

Burst capacity

Peak loads borrow from the pool; no single site has to over-provision.

Resilience

If one node drops, the others carry the load. No central point of failure.

Under the hood

Self-hosting, done properly.

Running a model locally is more than downloading weights. We engineer the inference and the memory so your private AI is fast, stable and accountable — on your own hardware.
