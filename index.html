Go beneath the word.

The meaning under any word or sentence. On your iPhone.

Coming to the App Store. No spam, one email.

Any word. Any sentence.

Type a whole sentence. OpenPoem returns what it really means, what it carries, and how it moves.

It all happens on your iPhone.

It runs on the model built into your iPhone. No account, no cloud, nothing leaves the device. It even works offline.
